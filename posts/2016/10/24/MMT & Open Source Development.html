MMT & Open Source Development

I've for a long time now had an idea for funding open source development but lacked a mental framework to describe and understand why it would work. As such I've not even been confident that it would work.

Today I just watch an excellent (EXCELLENT!) video on Modern Monetary Theory and Budget Deficits. I believe this provides the framework for analysing my idea for funding open source development. Unfortunately MMT is a very novel idea, and what is now my application of it to open source is itself quite strange so I think I needn't bother trying to cover everything in this single post.

It's late and I'm excited so I'll end this post here but I'll do a follow up post on the idea and possibly precede that with an intro to MMT.
